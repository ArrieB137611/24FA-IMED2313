In our solar system there are eight officialy recognized planets these are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. What about pluto, you might ask well it is recognized as one of the five dwarf planets, Ceres, Pluto, Haumea, Makemake, and Eris. A dwarf planet is a planetary mass that is in direct orbit of the sun but not at a size that would give orbital dominance.
